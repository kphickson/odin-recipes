Rice Salad

Easy to prepare vegetarian meal - use left overs or prepare beans and rice and add what you have on hand. Delicious served hot or cold. Make a big batch for a week's worth of lunches or use as a main dish for different meals throughout week.

Experiment with what is in season and what you have on hand. Try different flavor profiles!

  1. Make the base

    • Half Pound Dry Black Beans, cooked till soft, rinsed and drained
    • 3 Cups Brown rice, cooked
    • Place rice and beans in a large mixing bowl

  2. Add veggies

    • Chopped tomatoes, fresh or sun dried
    • Chopped onions
    • Frozen corn (hint: you only need to rinse frozen corn to use in recipes)
    • Chopped peppers - spicy, bell, banana
    • Chopped herbs (see below for flavor profiles)

  3. Add liquids

    • Vegetable stock or water

  4. Add Seasonings

    5. Try different mixes based on the flavor profile you are going for, adding your favorites. Suggestions below.

    • Mexican: cilantro, avocado, chili powder, cumin, red pepper, smoked paprika
    • Asian: soy sauce, sesame oil, red pepper flakes, garlic, coriander
    • Italian: olive oil, black olives, mushrooms, oregano, rosemary, basil
