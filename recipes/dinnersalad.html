Easy Weekday Dinner Salad

This is actually what is eaten, for dinner, on a daily basis around our house. Lots of variety and a healthy, easy choice for busy weekday nights when you don't want to think about cooking and just want to eat.

Prep takes about 15 minutes tops (depending on your knife skills)!

  1. Make the Base

      2. Romaine, greens, leaf mixes - the amount you use is based on your personal diet. For reference, for two, we split a large head of romaine. This is a dinner salad - not a puny side salad.

  2. Add veggies

      3. Based on what is in season. Some suggestions:
        Bell peppers - green, orange, red
        Other peppers - pablano, banana, serrano
        Carrots - shredded, sliced, chunky cut
        Radishes - daikon, sliced red radishes
        Tomatoes - cherry, heirloom, beefsteak
        Fruits - sectioned oranges, grapes

  3. Add starches

      4. Drained leftover or canned beans
      Roasted vegetables or potatoes
      Leftover rice

  4. Toppers

      5. Suggestions:
      Pickled: peppers, eggs, onions, okra
      Olives: green, black, kalamata
      Bottled: mushrooms, roasted peppers

  5. Salad dressing

    6. Bottled, oil and vinegar, homemade - whatever strikes your fancy

  6. Add crunchy stuff

      7. Whatever suits your tastes. Some suggestions:
      Roasted/salted sunflower seeds
      Almond slivers
      Cereal (example: Grape Nuts)
